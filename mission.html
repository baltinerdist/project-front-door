About the Order

Our mission

Amtgard is a game, a society, and a registered non-profit fellowship, and we hold all three to the same standard. What follows is who we are, in our own words.

i

Amtgard as a game

At its core, Amtgard is a Live-Action Roleplaying (LARP) and boffer-combat society focused on the Sword & Sorcery, Medieval and Ancient genres. We use foam-padded weapon replicas to physically enact fantasy scenarios — rather than describing actions, participants perform them. Swords are swung. Spells are cast.

Beyond combat, the system covers character classes, progression, magic, and special abilities. Weekly play advances levels, unlocks new capabilities, and the Dor Un Avathar and its five expansion books furnish over two hundred and fifty monsters and pre-built scenarios for tactical and roleplay engagement.

ii

Amtgard as a society

Amtgard operates without a predefined global narrative or storyline. This permits diverse character backgrounds spanning historical and fantasy traditions. Members adopt persona names ranging from the medieval to the contemporary fantasy.

A persona is a chosen identity, not a costume — and a persona name can be changed at any time, by any member, for any reason at all.

iii

Amtgard as a non-profit

Amtgard is a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit corporation. We receive technical and infrastructure support from organizations including UptimeRobot, Amazon Web Services, and CloudFlare, to whom we are indebted.

All play and participation is free. There are no dues required to walk onto a field. Optional dues exist for those who wish to vote in chapter governance or receive a printed rulebook, but they are never a barrier to entry.

Our standing oath

Inclusion is not a courtesy. It is a foundation.

Amtgard's leadership affirms that all people — regardless of gender, nationality, race, ethnicity, age, sexual orientation, mental or physical ability, or religious affiliation — should find Amtgard to be welcoming and inclusive.

We commit to active inclusion through our publications, our communications, our governance and our behavior, and we strive for both equality and equity in every park, kingdom, and tournament we hold.
