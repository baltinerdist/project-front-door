The Board of Directors

The current board, as of October 2025. The seat-holders below conduct the business of the order — chartering chapters, signing the conventions, and keeping the lights on. Their term is short, their work is constant, and their inboxes are open.

President

Lisa Belkie

Presiding over the order.

Treasurer

Jennifer Stewart

With Amtgard since 2016 as a resident of Thorn Mountain. She has held multiple internal club officer positions and spent over a decade managing an animal shelter and an animal services department. She currently serves on her local Animal Advisory Board as an ex officio member.

Secretary

Shannon Cartwright

Adjacent to the community since 2008, fully involved from 2020. Often serves as Prime Minister or on committees within Desert Winds. Her focus is helping future leadership teams and improving operations through strong relationship-building.

Board Member

Sonya Duran

Started in 2016. Sonya had their first major experience at Gathering of the Clans, doing volunteer work on their second day. Has autocrated several events and held positions at all organizational levels. Achieved Knight of the Flame in 2023 for volunteer contributions.

Board Member · Communications

Amber Hodges

A member for twenty-two-plus years, beginning in Burning Lands in Huntington, WV, then moving to Crystal Groves and Rising Winds. Served as park monarchy for most of her tenure, has worked as Ombudsman, and has held higher-office positions since September 2024. Outside the field, she volunteers with crisis intervention.

Executive Committee · Chair

Jason Bonette

Part of Amtgard since 1992. Played in the Wetlands and the Celestial Kingdom. Executive Chair of the Circle of Monarchs Committee since late 2019. Focuses on improving kingdom communication and keeping operations efficient.

Executive Committee · Secretary

Vidalia Darby

Maintains Executive Committee records and organisation. Precise in note-taking, sharp in addressing issues. Also a field fighter and a maker of art.

Executive Committee · Seat

Dale Churchill

Known on the field as Sir Ried, an active member since 2007. Has held Monarch, Champion, and Board President. Knighted for service and leadership, dedicated to strengthening parks and kingdoms through mentorship and event leadership.

General Counsel

J.P. Prentiss

Counsel to the order.
